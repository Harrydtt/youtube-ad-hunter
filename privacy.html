What This Extension Does

YouTube Focus Mode enhances your viewing experience by:

  • Optimizing video content delivery
  • Processing metadata for smoother playback
  • Cleaning up visual distractions on the page

Data Collection

This extension does NOT:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track browsing history
  • Send data to external servers
  • Store cookies or identifiers
  • Access account information

Local Storage

The extension only stores your preference settings locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Scripting: To optimize page content
  • Storage: To save your preferences
  • Offscreen: To process metadata in background

For questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
